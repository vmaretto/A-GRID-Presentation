Problem Statement

Current Challenges

  • Lack of transparency in the agricultural supply chain
  • Inefficiencies in tracking and verifying the origin and quality of produce
  • Food safety concerns due to limited traceability
  • Difficulty in verifying sustainable and organic farming practices

Consumer Demand

  • Increasing demand for organic, local, and sustainable food products [5]
  • Growing concern for food safety and quality
  • Desire for transparency in food sourcing and production methods

UAE Organic Food Market Growth [5]

The UAE organic food market is projected to experience significant growth:

Projected growth from USD 42.57 million in 2023 to USD 55.63 million by 2029

Opportunity

  • Address the gap between consumer demand for transparent, sustainable food and current supply chain limitations
  • Leverage blockchain technology to provide end-to-end traceability and verify organic certification
  • Tap into the growing organic food market in the UAE
