Business Model

Revenue Streams [8]

  • Subscription-based access: Tiered pricing for access to the blockchain platform, catering to different sizes and needs of agricultural operations [8]
  • Transaction fees: Small fees for smart contract execution and blockchain transactions [8]
  • Premium services: Advanced analytics, customized reports, and consulting services for enterprise clients [8]

Scalability [8]

  • Platform designed to accommodate diverse needs of both small farms and large agricultural operations
  • Modular architecture allows for easy integration of new features and technologies
  • Cloud-based infrastructure enables rapid scaling to meet growing demand

Value Proposition [8]

  • Increased transparency and trust in the supply chain
  • Improved operational efficiency through automation and real-time data access
  • Enhanced food safety and quality assurance
  • Access to new markets and premium pricing for verified sustainable products

Customer Segments [8]

  • Small to medium-sized farms
  • Large agricultural enterprises
  • Food processors and distributors
  • Retailers and supermarket chains
  • Regulatory bodies and certification agencies
